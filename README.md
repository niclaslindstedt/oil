# oil

A TUI CLI that visualizes the price of the important oil in the world. Sourcing data from free data sources.

[![CI](https://github.com/niclaslindstedt/oil/actions/workflows/ci.yml/badge.svg)](https://github.com/niclaslindstedt/oil/actions/workflows/ci.yml)
[![License: MIT](https://img.shields.io/badge/license-MIT-blue.svg)](LICENSE)

## Why?

- Stay ahead of fuel and transport costs without leaving your terminal.
- Make faster trading or budgeting decisions with real-time price context at a glance.
- No API keys or paid subscriptions — zero friction from install to insight.
- Keep an eye on global market signals without switching to a browser mid-workflow.
- Spot trends across crude benchmarks in seconds, not after hunting through multiple data sites.
